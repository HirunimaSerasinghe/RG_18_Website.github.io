About Us

Our Features

Train Booking

The app allows users to book train tickets conveniently from their mobile devices, eliminating the need to wait in long queues at ticket counters. This saves time and provides a more user-friendly experience.

Fingerprint Payment and QR Ticket

Our app introduces fingerprint payment and QR code tickets, providing a secure and seamless ticketing experience. With fingerprint authentication, users can make payments with ease, while digital QR codes replace traditional tickets for convenient access.

Real-Time Train Tracking

The app provides real-time information on train locations, schedules, and delays. Passengers can track the exact location of their train, plan their journey accordingly, and avoid unnecessary waiting at the station.
